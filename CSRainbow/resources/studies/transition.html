Puberty Blockers: A Review of GnRH Analogues in Transgender Youth

A review of research on puberty blockers for transgender youth. It finds they are helpful with minor risks.

A study found that puberty blockers decreased emotional and behavioral problems compaired to transgender youth who did not get treatment and a cisgender control group.

Gender identity and transition: relationships with sleep disorders in US youth
------
A study found that transgender youth have more sleep disorders and that having gender affirming care is linked with less of them.


Continuation of gender-affirming hormones in transgender people starting puberty suppression in adolescence: a cohort study in the Netherlands>
------
A study of 720 transgender youth in the Netherlands found that 98% of people continued treatment into adulthood. This shows regret rates from gender-afferming care are likely quite low.


Hormonal therapy and sex reassignment: a systematic review and meta-analysis of quality of life and psychosocial outcomes
------
A review of 28 studies found that most transgender people had less gender dyphoria and better mental health after HRT and surgry that was around the population baseline.
