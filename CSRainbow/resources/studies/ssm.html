Same sex marriage


What does the scholarly research say about the well-being of children with gay or lesbian parents?
------
A analysis of 79 studies that studied same sex parenting. 75 of those studies found no negative affect while the other 4 were found to have methodological issues.


The Effects of Marriage, Civil Union, and Domestic Partnership Laws on the Health and Well-being of Children
------
A study that reviewers the literature around same sex parenting found no negative affect on children


Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgender (LGBT) Parented Families A Literature Review prepared for The Australian Psychological Society
------
A study that reviewed the literature around same sex parenting found no negative affect on children


Difference-in-Differences Analysis of the Association Between State Same-Sex Marriage Policies and Adolescent Suicide Attempts
------
A study found that states with same sex marriage policies had lower suicide rates among sexual orientation minority youth.
