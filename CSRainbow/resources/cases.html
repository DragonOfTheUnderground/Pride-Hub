US LGBT+ Rights Cases

This is a work in progress list of cases concerning LGBT+ rights in the United States. We will expand the list over time. Some of these cases are positive and some are negative. This list is not comprehesive and mainly covers big cases.


Gender-affirming care

Doe v. Ladapo - challenging Flordia's gender-affirming care ban for transgender youth (GLAD Gender Analysis)

Dekker v. Weida - challenging Flordia's removal of gender-affirming care coverage (Lambda Legal Gender Analysis)

Brandt v. Griffin - challenging Arkansas's gender-affirming care ban for transgender youth (ACLU Gender Analysis Court Listener)

Boe v. Marshall - challenging Alabama's gender-affirming care ban for transgender youth (GLAD Court Listener)

PFLAG v. Abbott - challenging Texas's attempt to make gender affirming care child abuse (Lambda Legal ACLU)


Education

Equality Florida v. Flordia State Board Of Education - challenging Flordia's "don't say gay" law (Equality Flordia Court Listener)

Cousins v. The School Board of Orange County - challenging Flordia's "don't say gay" law (Lambda Legal)


Discrimination

303 Creative LLC v. Elenis - challenging Colorado's LGBT+ nondiscrimination law (SCOTUS Blog)


Transgender student sports

B.P.J. v. West Virginia State Board of Education - challenging West Virginia's transgender student sports ban (ACLU)


Drag show restrictions

Friends of Georges, Inc. v. State of Tennessee - challenging Tennessee's drag show restriction law (Court Listener)
